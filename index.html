About

This website showcases work from the Data Journalism course at the Hertie School in Berlin (Spring 2026). Students produced short takes — focused data journalism pieces — and a final project over the course of the semester. The projects span investigative data work, interactive visualizations, and explanatory journalism.

The course was taught by Prof. Simon Munzert. Course materials are openly available on GitHub. Each project is featured here with the explicit consent of its authors and remains under their control.

About the course

GRAD-E1493: Data Journalism is taught by Prof. Simon Munzert at the Hertie School, Berlin. Topics covered include web scraping, survey analysis, maps and geodata, data visualization, and interactive graphics.
